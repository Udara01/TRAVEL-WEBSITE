Polonnaruwa..Historic Sri Lankan Gem..

Polonnaruwa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Sri Lanka, was once the majestic royal capital, now a showcase of ancient ruins revealing its glorious past. Its temples, stupas, palaces, and irrigation systems speak volumes of its rich history. Visitors wander through this sprawling complex, marveling at architectural marvels, immersing themselves in the city's bygone era. With serene lakes and lush greenery, Polonnaruwa offers a captivating journey through time, leaving an unforgettable impression on all who visit..
